Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

SynVision ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

SynVision is designed with privacy in mind. All training data and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any external servers.

Local Network Access

SynVision requests local network access permission to:

  • Discover other SynVision devices on the same WiFi network
  • Establish connections between your iPhone (controller) and iPad (display)

This communication occurs only within your local network and no data is sent to external servers.

Location Access & Wi-Fi Information

SynVision requests location permission solely to:

  • Access the Wi-Fi name (SSID) of your current connection on iOS.
  • Ensure both the phone and tablet are on the same local network for stable data synchronization.

We guarantee: This information is used only locally for network validation. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your geographic location data.

Camera Access

SynVision may request camera access to scan QR codes for quick device pairing. Camera data is processed locally and is never stored or transmitted.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Training records and statistics
  • App settings and preferences
  • Device connection history

Third-Party Services

SynVision does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

SynVision is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: synvision.app@gmail.com
